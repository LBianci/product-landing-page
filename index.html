Introduction

The Moka Pot is the perfect stovetop coffee maker for anyone who loves rich, bold espresso-style coffee. A staple in Italian households, the Moka Pot is as stylish as it is functional.

How to Use

  • Fill the bottom chamber with water up to the safety valve.
  • Add ground coffee to the filter basket without tamping.
  • Assemble the Moka Pot and place it on medium heat.
  • Wait for the hissing sound, indicating your coffee is ready.
  • Remove from heat, pour, and enjoy!
